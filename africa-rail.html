Overview
Connecting Africa

CIG is a company dedicated to developing large-scale railway infrastructure across the African continent. Its mission is to connect strategic regions in order to facilitate the efficient transport of natural resources and goods. By leveraging modern technologies and strong partnerships, CIG actively contributes to Africa's economic transformation. The company positions itself as a key player in advancing regional market integration.

Through its trans-African railway projects, CIG aims to optimize logistics chains linked to mining, agriculture, and energy sectors. By reducing transportation costs and improving the flow of goods, these infrastructures enhance the competitiveness of local economies. CIG's approach is built on a sustainable vision, combining economic performance with environmental responsibility. Each project is designed to deliver long-term positive impact.

CIG also plays a strategic role in strengthening economic integration across the continent by facilitating intra-African trade. By connecting landlocked regions to major trade corridors, the company supports community development and access to new opportunities. Its ambition is to build a modern, reliable, and interconnected rail network capable of supporting Africa's future growth. Through this vision, CIG contributes to shaping a more connected and prosperous future.
